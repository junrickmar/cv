Mechanical Plant Supervisor in La Suerte Cigar and Cigarrette Factory.

  • Start in Feb. 2015 up to Present

Key responsibilities:

  • Conducts the daily inspection of all machinery and equipment, making sure that these are in good working condition
  • Schedules the maintenance and repair of all plant machinery and equipment.
  • Assigns, supervises and evaluates the work of the Maintenance personnel.
  • Troubleshoots machinery and equipment problems.
  • Requests and follows up, if necessary, the outside/technical services and additional tools needed by the Maintenance Section.
  • Coordinates with the machine shops for the fabrication of machine parts.
  • Reports machinery/equipment failure and the status of machinery/equipment maintenance and repair work to the Plant Manager.
